How To Increase Brand Awareness

By Pier Solutions

Featuring Lance St.Vil

Creating a strong brand, social media presence, and an overall reputation is key to creating a stream for brand awareness.

Nike Logo, recognizable brand logo.

Brand Awareness is key to creating a well known, powerful reputation for you and your company. You don't want to be unheard of, have no social presence and not be creating a unique brand for your company.

Creating your own personal, unique content and branding your company and a service or product that offers exclusivity and nothing like anyone has seen before is what differentiates good and bad brand awareness

From Search Engine Optimization (SEO) to blogs and websites, forums, social media streams. All of these are great examples of creating a good stream of brand awareness. er again is the first step.

After reaching out to Lance St.Vil who is a dynamic, passionate, professional, self-motivated content writer, SEO article writer, editor, website content writer and social media manager, I was able to gather some real insight to what he does to create a reputation and show his exclusivity. Below you will find what he does, which you can also use to create exclusivity and uniqueness as a person or brand.

Creating Unique Content

"The most important thing I focus on when publishing content is the content; subheadings; keywords, title, and overall competitor analysis. I use advanced research techniques to conduct original research and organize content in a structured manner, making it relevant and immediately usable. What that means is, I turn words into pretty pictures."

Creating Blog Posts

"The most important thing when writing a blog post and publishing specific content is SEO. As a professional, it's your job to direct traffic. Blog articles educate and help spread brand awareness. In an attempt to increase brand or product awareness, I proactively use SEO to drive more traffic to your blog and help secure Newsletter subscribers."

The Importance Of Ads

"Third-party apps or ADS help small to medium-sized businesses extend their reach. I've seen variations of this; affiliate links and paid advertisements. If done correctly, and if the ADS are targeted correctly to the right audience, it proves to be a success almost all the time."

Gaining Clients Organically

"The ways that I gain clients organically are:

1. Social Media (Instagram, Facebook user groups)

2. Blogs - SEO blogs

3. Paid Ads ( on Facebook Pinterest or Instagram)

4. Marketing services on forums like Quora"

You can see that creating brand awareness is key to a successful business. Following these steps above will only prove successful and will in the end show customers that you are reliable, reputable, and respectable. Creating Social Streams, Using SEO, creating a website, and so many more things you can do will all help you to increase and drive business in the right direction.
