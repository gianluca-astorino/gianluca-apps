How To Get Your First Customers

By Pier Solutions

Getting your first customers, your FIRST SALE! The feeling is like nothing before. But it's a lot harder then it seems.

For us, finding customers who are interested in buying a service is hard, especially if you don't have a huge portfolio or a great media presence. That's one reason right away why you should build your social presence. You can learn more here.

Ads, Word of mouth, organic social reach, SEO, so many more ways to gain attention. But what's the REAL SECRET to gaining your first customers? Well i'm about to tell you.

After listening to an informative YouTube livestream by Chris Hughes, I learned a few things.

Simple but useful ways to gain your first customers include:

Cold Calling

Cold Calling, something that is often overlooked. Almost nothing is more beneficial then cold calling people, small businesses, big companies, etc. It gives you the chance to tweak what you are saying, how you are pitching your service, how to gain their attention, and the possibility of gaining a new customer and getting your name out there.

Start With Who You Know

Family, friends, friends of friends. You should be looking for these opportunities to build your reputation. For Example, if you build a website for a business your friend owns, they are going to get asked who built their website, they are going to spread the word for you, which in return is essentially free advertising. Word Of Mouth is extremely powerful.

Social Media Presence

Building yourself an online presence is something that is the CHEAPEST form of advertising and will help build your online reputation. You can read more about it here.

As you can see, it isn't easy, it'll take time, but like anything else, you must put time into it, put out good, quality content that people look for, and just contact as many people as you can.
