How To Increase Social Presence

By Pier Solutions

In today’s day and age, it is more important than ever to have a HUGE social presence.

I cannot name a single person who doesn’t Google a business, or look for social media accounts that provide related info to that business. Being live, present and alive on social media is key to success.

Now i’m not just talking about a website, although that is one of, if not the most important strategy to attract customers and give users a base to check you out, but all social media is important, from Facebook to Instagram, Medium and Twitter.

Instagram is one of the easiest, cheapest most convenient ways to attract customers. When we start working with customers, we always recommend one of the first things they do before we even get started on building their website.

One of our recent customers, a private swimming teacher, sat down with us and we explained to them the importance of a Google Business Listing, growing your instagram account, getting out onto Facebook and Twitter, posting local ads, etc. It is one of the most important things when starting a business to get online as much as possible, it should be a large focus in a startup.

ADS

Ads in my opinion work well if your starting out. If you can get FREE ads, even better, but most aren’t free. Typically accounts with 100k followers or more will sell you ads for roughly $10–$20 which is very good price.

Facebook ads work good as well, but tend to be a bit more pricey, usually $25-$30, which isn’t much more but if your on a tight budget, $10-$15 in saving could be helpful.

Google Business Listings

Google business listing is extremely helpful in making a business more professional. It gives your customers or new customers the sense that they know that you’re legit, your not going to scam them, and give them more reassurance your professional and know what you’re doing. You can google my Google My Business or download the app for more info.

Instagram

Instagram in our opinion is very important, one of the most important social media applications and is a great marketing strategy. With over 1 billion active users monthly, Instagram gives you a large base for reaching out to customers, and posting high quality content to show your customers what you’re all about.

Like I said before, you should always try and get out onto Social Media as much as possible, it lets customers know who you are and what kind of work you do. It’s a very critical strategy to succeed when starting a business
