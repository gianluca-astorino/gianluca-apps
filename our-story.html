OUR STORY

OUR NAME, OUR MEANING

WHAT WE REPRESENT

PIER SOLUTIONS represents something more then just a business, a website developer and design business. Our name represents a greater meaning. Our name came about to be because as many people travel to beaches, they come across a pier, one where you can walk to the end and appreciate the beautiful ocean and life as you take in your surrondings. Reaching out into the open water and standing at the edge of that pier makes you feel as though you can do anything, you have the world at your fingertips, as if you are reaching out and grabbing your dreams. We feel that our name represents just that, and that is our story. We want to provide more then just a service for developing websites. We want to and are providing all of our customers with an experience, a growing friendship, more then anything another company could offer. Thats just who we are, and what we do.
