Is now the right time to start a business?

By Pier Solutions

Starting a business, ahh every kids dream. Everyone dreams about financial freedom, having money to spend whenever and wherever you want and not having to worry about whether your spending to much and if you can afford it.

Some people dream about the vacations to Hawaii and the Maldives, the weekend getaways, the luxury cars, the huge houses in the mountains of Malibu, and a vacation homes in Monaco. I know I do.

But starting a business isn’t just all that. It requires that extra effort that most people don’t want to give. There is 1% of people who will put in the extra effort, the 1% who rise above everyone else.

During this COVID-19 world pandemic, thousands die, the stock market continues to tank, the real estate market isn’t the greatest, and millions lose their job. Yet we’re here talking about if it’s the right time to start a business?

Well there isn’t a set time that anyone should start your business, but right now seems like the perfect time, doesn’t it?

As most work from home, people have extra time on their hands, and what better opportunity to get a start your dream job, get yourself on track to the financial freedom you’ve been dreaming of since a kid, those unlimited world travels and laying beachside while doing emails and having a computer on your lap to do work. Nothing sounds better then that does it.

Rushes of excitement, stomach dropping happiness, early mornings, late nights, calling every person and business you possibly can to try and sell your product(s) or service are the best part of starting your business. Building connections, creating a brand for yourself, making your first ever sale. Boy oh boy are those exciting times.

It’s times like these that motivate us to do better, to start our own journey to success. With money tight, job losses, stocks down, extra time, and so many more reasons, now could not be a better time to start your business.
